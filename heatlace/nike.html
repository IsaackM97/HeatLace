Nike Collab

Tinker Hatfield

Nike has been a renowned sneaker brand that has caught the attention of designers and brands to help colaborate on variety of sneaker and clothing projects. Tinker hatfield has established himself as the father of the Nike Air Max. He helped develop and design many of the early models for Nike and has also worked alongside Jordan Brand.

Round Two

Round Two is a streetwear and vintage clothing store that was originally founded in Virginia. Currently has stores in Miami, New York, Chicago and Los Angeles. One of the founders Sean Wotherspoon, was able to design his own Air Max and his design won, so his design became the shoe for Air Max Day (March 26th) 2017.

Virgil Abloh

Virgil Abloh has become one of the biggest names in fashion over the past 10 years. He started off by creating his own clothing brand Pyrex, which was later called Off-White. Through his connection with Kanye West and Nike. He was able to design his own twist on the vast collection of models that Nike has made over the years. Models such as the Air Force 1, Nike Vapormax, Air Max 90, and Nike Blazer are some of the models he put his creative touch on.

Atmos

Atmos is a sneaker store that is based in Tokyo, Japan. This store has a long history of being one of the first sneaker stores in Tokyo. The owner of Atmos named Hidefumi Hommyo, has built a long and healthy relationship with Nike. So much, that Nike allowed Atmos to deisgn their own Air Max 1, Air Max 90, and Air Max 95.

Kith

Kith is a clothing store that was founded in New York City by Ronnie Fieg. His stored was launched on November 2011. He has created his own brand from the ground up making numerous collaborations with these Nike models: Air Force 1, Nike Air Max 90, and Nike Air Maestro.

Patta X Parra

Parra is a Dutch artist who teamed up with the Amsterdam boutique store Patta. Where they would unveil the exclusive Air Max 1 collaboration with Nike in 2010. The sneaker is considered to be one of the "grails" that many sneakerheads are hoping to have in their colection one day.
