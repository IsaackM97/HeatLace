The Jordan Effect

Goal Driven

Michael Jordan was a man on a mission to leave his mark on the NBA. In the start of his basketball career he was denied a spot on his basketball varsity High School team. From this experience, he was determined to prove those who did not beleive in his ability to be great at basketball.

Winner Mindset

When Mike was at the University of North Carolina, he promised his team and coach that he will lead the team to a national title and he did. When he was with the Chicago Bulls, he promised the city a national title and instead of one championship. Jordan went above and beyond and won six titles with the Bulls.

Streetwear Fashion

Through the pedigree of Jordan as a brand, many fashion designers were influenced by the design of his sneakers and created clothing pieces that best fit the asthetic of his shoes. The most popular model being the Air Jordan 1. Many hip hop artists have also took influence of Jordan brand and some artists have joined the brand. The most current and popular artist being Travis Scott.

Spike Lee

Spike Lee played a huge part in advertising Jordan's sneakers. One of his most iconic commercials consisted of him and Jordan shooting the basketball around and telling Mike that it has to be the shoes giving him the skill.

Travis Scott

Travis Scott was at the peak of his career during the time of his album Astroworld and it only got better for him. He has mentioned that his favorite brand of sneakers were Nikes. Through this, he created a relationship with Jordan brand.

A$AP Rocky

ASAP Rocky is a massive fashion icon in the streetwear and designer industry. Many of his fans come from his hip hop group named A$AP Mob. Where many of the members of the group have contributed to the streetwear culture.
